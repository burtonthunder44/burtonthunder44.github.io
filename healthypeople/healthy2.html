Research Center

At our research center, we have availble spots for internships for high school and college students that are in the small group program. You will learn more about mental health, research about it, help with the counselor's small groups, and share your stories with them. You will learn how it impacts others, about healthy coping mechanims, and how it affects toddlers to adults.
